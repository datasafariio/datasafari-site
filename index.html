Core identity

A Tanzanian NGO building practical data capability.

Data Safari exists for learners who have ambition but need access, structure, mentors, tools, and a community that believes their questions matter.

Mission

Our mission is to utilize the power of data to make informed decisions and solve community problems.

Vision

Our vision is a world where data-driven solutions are used to improve human welfare and sustain our planet.

Headquarters

Morogoro

Data Safari is headquartered in Morogoro and rooted in Tanzania's education and community needs.

Students

Tanzania Mainland

Access

Educational roadmap

Three programs. One continuous path.

The roadmap starts with data literacy in secondary schools, grows into AI literacy in universities, and advances toward industrial Data Science and AI practice.

Data Wizards

A foundational Data Science program for secondary school students, designed to build data literacy and confidence early.

Secondary school Foundational
  • ConceptsData, data collection, analysis, visualization, and asking useful questions.
  • ToolsExcel, Python, and Jupyter Notebooks for hands-on classroom learning.
  • GoalMake Data Science feel local, practical, and possible before university.
Ask about Data Wizards →

AI Wizards

A foundational AI literacy program for university students from all professional backgrounds.

4 universities 60 students Active cohort
  • ConceptsData collection and preprocessing, data analysis, ML lifecycle, model development, evaluation, and improvement.
  • AIModel architectures, deep learning, and large language models such as GPTs.
  • ToolsNo-code and low-code practice with Orange Data Mining and Google's Teachable Machine.

AI Bootcamp

An advanced Data Science and AI training program now in development, designed to build on AI Wizards.

Advanced In development
  • StackPython, NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow/Keras, PyTorch, and associated models.
  • PracticeIndustrial-standard workflows for data preparation, modeling, evaluation, and iteration.

Operational principles

The values behind every class, club, and project.

These four principles guide how Data Safari teaches, collaborates, raises awareness, and turns technology into social impact.

01

Education

We provide high-quality programming lessons, training, and resources for learners starting their data journey.

02

Community Engagement

We build an inclusive and supportive community where learners connect, share, and learn from each other.

03

Sensitization

We raise awareness in local communities, promote digital literacy, and help bridge the digital divide.

04

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and experimentation to address pressing social, environmental, and economic issues.

Target audience and reach

Built in Morogoro. Focused across Tanzania.

Data Safari's current work centers on secondary school and university students throughout Tanzania, with programs designed for practical learning and community problem-solving.

  • Secondary schools: Data Wizards introduces learners to data, collection, analysis, visualization, Excel, Python, and Jupyter Notebooks.
  • Universities: AI Wizards clubs bring AI literacy to students from science, business, health, agriculture, education, and other fields.
  • Morogoro headquarters: The organization is based in Morogoro and operates across Tanzania Mainland.
  • Community problem-solving: Learners connect data skills to issues that affect real people, local systems, and the planet.
