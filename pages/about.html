Our story

The WHY Gives You The HOW!

We are a registered Tanzanian NGO on a mission to make data science and AI education accessible to every learner — from Morogoro's secondary schools to universities across the nation.

Core identity

A Tanzanian NGO building practical data capability.

Data Safari exists for learners who have ambition but need access, structure, mentors, tools, and a community that believes their questions matter.

Partner with Us →

Mission

Our mission is to utilise the power of data to make informed decisions and solve community problems. We deliver this through structured, progressive education programmes rooted in Tanzanian contexts.

Vision

A Tanzania where every student — regardless of location, gender, or socioeconomic background — can access world-class data science and AI education and apply it to the problems they care about.

Registered NGO

Data Safari is a formally registered non-governmental organisation headquartered in Morogoro, Tanzania, operating with full transparency, community accountability, and an open-source ethos.

Our journey

From founding to 3,000+ students.

2022

Data Safari Founded

Data Safari is established as a registered NGO in Morogoro, Tanzania. HQ set up, founding team assembled, initial curriculum developed.

2023

First Data Wizards Cohort

120 secondary school students complete the inaugural 12-week Data Wizards programme. Community data projects presented to local government leaders in Morogoro.

2024

AI Wizards University Clubs

AI Wizards clubs launched at 3 universities across Tanzania. 60 students in the first cohort build no-code AI models using Orange Data Mining and Teachable Machine.

2025

First AI Bootcamp

The inaugural 3-day AI Bootcamp produces 200 graduates who deploy working machine learning models. Partnerships expand to 4 universities. Curriculum open-sourced on GitHub.

2026

3,000+ Students Reached

Data Safari now active in 5 regions across Tanzania mainland. 3,000+ students reached across all three programmes. New cohorts forming — join the movement.

What we believe

Principles that guide every decision.

🌍

Access for All

Quality data education should not be limited by geography, income, or gender. We design for the margins first and work outward.

🔬

Real Data, Real Problems

Every exercise uses Tanzanian data about Tanzanian realities. Students work on problems they actually care about solving.

🤝

Community-First

Insights belong to the communities they describe. We train students to share findings, not hoard them, and to listen as much as they measure.

📖

Open Source

All curricula, datasets, and tools are open-source and freely available. We build on the shoulders of the global open-source community and give back to it.

⚖️

Ethical AI

Every programme includes dedicated modules on AI ethics, bias, fairness, and responsible data use — because the WHY always comes before the HOW.

🚀

Progressive Mastery

Our three-tier ladder meets every learner where they are — from first spreadsheet to deployed model — with a clear, achievable path forward.
